Our Journey

From a simple idea to Australia's most-loved skincare destination. This is the PINK story.

Great skin shouldn't be a luxury. PINK was born from the belief that every Australian deserves access to the world's best skincare — without paying a fortune. What started as one person's frustration with overpriced beauty products became a mission to democratise premium skincare.

January 2026
💡

The Idea

Darren Smith noticed Australians were paying 30-50% more for skincare compared to global prices. He researched direct supplier partnerships and envisioned an online store that could bring CeraVe, The Ordinary, and Drunk Elephant to Australia at fair prices.

February 2026
🔨

Building PINK

Partnered with CJDropshipping and authorised skincare distributors worldwide. Built the platform using DevBot AI for automated inventory, pricing, and customer support. Curated the initial product range — 35 of the world's most popular skincare products.

March 2026
🌸

Launch Day

PINK goes live with 35+ products from 15+ trusted brands. Free express shipping over $79 AUD. The response is immediate — skincare lovers across Australia discover a new way to shop for their favourite products.

March 2026
💕

Community Grows

800+ customers in the first month. 4.8-star average rating. Word of mouth spreads as customers share their hauls on Instagram and TikTok. PINK becomes synonymous with authentic, affordable skincare in Australia.

Q2 2026 — Coming Soon
✨

Expanding the Range

Adding hair care, body care, and wellness products. PINK-branded skincare tools launching. Loyalty rewards program and subscription boxes in development.

Q3 2026 — Vision
🌏

Going International

Expansion to New Zealand and Southeast Asia. Collaborations with Australian skincare influencers. PINK-exclusive product bundles and curated routines.

2027 — The Dream
🏪

Physical Pop-Ups

PINK experience stores in Adelaide and Melbourne. Skin consultations, product testing, and community events. 500+ products. Australia's #1 independent skincare retailer.
