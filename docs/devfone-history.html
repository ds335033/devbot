Our Journey

From a bold idea in South Australia to Australia's favourite tech store. This is the DEVFONE story.

Every great company starts somewhere. DEVFONE started with a simple belief: Australians deserve better access to premium technology without the premium price tag. What began as one person's frustration with overpriced electronics became a mission to change how Australia shops for tech.

January 2026
💡

The Spark

Darren Smith, a tech enthusiast from South Australia, noticed Australians were consistently paying 20-40% more for the same electronics compared to global prices. He envisioned a store that connects directly with global suppliers — cutting out the middlemen and passing savings to customers.

February 2026
🔨

Building the Foundation

Partnered with CJDropshipping and established direct supplier relationships across Asia. Built the DEVFONE platform from scratch using custom AI-powered technology — DevBot — an engine that automates inventory, pricing, customer support, and order fulfilment. Set up affiliate partnerships with Apple, Amazon AU, and JB Hi-Fi.

March 2026
🚀

Going Live

DEVFONE officially launches with 50+ premium products spanning iPhones, MacBooks, Samsung Galaxy, AirPods, wearables, and 37 high-margin accessories. First orders ship from South Australia. Free express shipping over $99 becomes a cornerstone of the brand.

March 2026
🎉

First 1,000 Customers

Community grows rapidly through word of mouth, social media, and competitive pricing. Average customer rating hits 4.7 stars. The combination of fast Australian shipping, genuine products, and rock-bottom prices resonates with tech-savvy Australians across the country.

Q2 2026 — Coming Soon
🎮

Expanding the Range

Plans to add gaming peripherals, smart home devices, drone accessories, and a DEVFONE-branded accessories line. Mobile app development begins for iOS and Android. Exploring same-day metro delivery partnerships.

Q3 2026 — Vision
🌏

Going International

Expansion to New Zealand and Southeast Asia. Localised stores for each market. The DEVFONE branded accessories line launches — premium quality at disruptive prices, designed in Australia.

2027 — The Future
🏪

Physical Presence

Pop-up experience stores in Adelaide and Melbourne. 10,000+ products across all categories. Australia's #1 independent electronics retailer. The mission complete — premium tech, fair prices, for everyone.

Our Philosophy

Three principles that guide everything at DEVFONE.

🎯

Direct to Consumer

We cut out the middlemen. By partnering directly with manufacturers and global suppliers, we eliminate unnecessary markups. The savings go straight to you — not to a chain of distributors.

🤖

AI-Powered Operations

DevBot — our custom AI engine — manages inventory, optimises pricing in real-time, automates order fulfilment, and powers 24/7 customer support. Technology running on technology.

💬

Community First

Every product we add, every feature we build, and every policy we create is shaped by customer feedback. Our community of tech enthusiasts drives the direction of DEVFONE.

"I started DEVFONE because I believed Australians shouldn't have to choose between quality and affordability. Every product we sell, every partnership we build, and every customer we serve brings us closer to that mission. This is just the beginning."
— Darren Smith, Founder & CEO of DEVFONE
