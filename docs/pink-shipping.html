FAQ

Common shipping questions answered.

When will my order ship?▼

Orders placed before 2pm ACST Mon-Fri ship same day. Weekend orders ship Monday.

Are skincare products packaged safely?▼

Yes — all products are wrapped in bubble wrap with temperature-safe packaging. Serums and glass bottles get extra protection.

Can I change my delivery address?▼

Yes, if your order hasn't shipped yet. Email hello@pinkbeauty.store with your order number.

What if my product arrives damaged?▼

Contact us within 7 days with photos. We'll send a replacement or full refund — no questions asked.

Do you ship internationally?▼

Yes! We ship to NZ and worldwide. International customs duties are the buyer's responsibility.

Returns & Exchanges

Changed your mind? PINK offers 30-day hassle-free returns on all unopened products in original condition. We cover return shipping for faulty or damaged items.
