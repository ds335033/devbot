Returns & Refunds

We want you to love your purchase. If something isn't right, we'll make it right.

Updated: 28 March 2026

↩️ Return Policy Overview

30-Day Hassle-Free Returns — Return any item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange, no questions asked.

At DEVFONE, we stand behind every product we sell. Our return policy covers all products purchased directly through our store (dropship items). Affiliate purchases are subject to the respective retailer's return policy.

📋 Return Conditions

Eligible for Return

  • Items within 30 days of delivery date
  • Unused, undamaged items in original packaging
  • Items with all tags, accessories, and documentation included
  • Defective or damaged items (no time limit for manufacturer defects)

Not Eligible for Return

  • Screen protectors that have been applied/opened
  • Earbuds/headphones with broken hygiene seal
  • Items purchased via affiliate links (Apple, Amazon, JB Hi-Fi) — contact the retailer directly
  • Items damaged through misuse or neglect

💰 Refund Process

Step 1: Email returns@devfone.store with your order number and reason for return.

Step 2: We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label within 24 hours.

Step 3: Pack the item securely and drop it at your nearest post office.

Step 4: Once received and inspected (1-2 business days), your refund is processed.

Refund Timing: Refunds are issued to your original payment method within 5-10 business days after we receive the item.

🔄 Exchanges

Want a different size, colour, or product? We offer free exchanges on all eligible items. Email us at returns@devfone.store and we'll ship the replacement as soon as we receive your return.

🇦🇺 Australian Consumer Law

Our return policy operates alongside your rights under Australian Consumer Law. You are entitled to a replacement or refund for a major failure and compensation for any other reasonably foreseeable loss or damage. You are also entitled to have the goods repaired or replaced if goods fail to be of acceptable quality and the failure does not amount to a major failure.
