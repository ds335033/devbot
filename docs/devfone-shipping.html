Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DEVFONE shipping.

When will my order ship?▼

Orders placed before 2pm ACST Monday-Friday are processed same day. Weekend orders ship on Monday. You'll receive a tracking number by email as soon as your order is dispatched.

Can I change my delivery address after ordering?▼

Yes — as long as your order hasn't shipped yet. Email support@devfone.store with your order number and new address. Once shipped, address changes are not possible.

What if my package is lost or damaged?▼

All shipments are insured. If your package is lost or arrives damaged, contact us within 7 days. We'll arrange a full replacement or refund — no questions asked.

Do you ship to PO boxes?▼

Yes, we ship to PO boxes via Australia Post for standard-size items. Oversized items (laptops, monitors) require a street address for courier delivery.

Are there customs or duties on international orders?▼

International orders may be subject to local customs duties and import taxes. These are the responsibility of the recipient and vary by country. DEVFONE does not collect international duties at checkout.

Can I pick up my order in-store?▼

We're currently online-only based in South Australia. Click & collect may be available in the future as we expand.

Returns & Exchanges

Changed your mind? No worries. DEVFONE offers a 30-day hassle-free return policy on all products. Items must be in original condition with packaging. We'll cover the return shipping for faulty items.
