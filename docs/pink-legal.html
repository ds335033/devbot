Returns & Refunds

Your skin deserves the best. If something isn't right, we'll make it right.

Updated: 28 March 2026

↩️ Return Policy Overview

30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee — Return any unopened item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

At PINK, we stand behind every product we sell. Our return policy covers all skincare products purchased directly through our store.

📋 Return Conditions

Eligible for Return

  • Unopened, sealed items within 30 days of delivery date
  • Items in original packaging with all seals intact
  • Defective or damaged items (no time limit for manufacturer defects)
  • Items that caused an allergic reaction (with photo evidence)

Not Eligible for Return

  • Opened or used skincare products (hygiene reasons)
  • Products with broken seals or tampered packaging
  • Gift cards or promotional items
  • Items damaged through improper storage (e.g., heat exposure)
Allergic Reaction? If you experience an adverse reaction, contact us immediately. We accept returns on opened products in cases of allergic reactions with a doctor's note or photo evidence.

💰 Refund Process

Step 1: Email returns@pinkbeauty.com.au with your order number and reason for return.

Step 2: We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label within 24 hours.

Step 3: Pack the item securely and drop it at your nearest post office.

Step 4: Once received and inspected (1-2 business days), your refund is processed.

Refund Timing: Refunds are issued to your original payment method within 5-10 business days after we receive the item.

🔄 Exchanges

Want to swap for a different product or shade? We offer free exchanges on all eligible items. Email us at returns@pinkbeauty.com.au and we'll ship the replacement as soon as we receive your return.

🇦🇺 Australian Consumer Law

Our return policy operates alongside your rights under Australian Consumer Law. You are entitled to a replacement or refund for a major failure and compensation for any other reasonably foreseeable loss or damage. You are also entitled to have the goods repaired or replaced if goods fail to be of acceptable quality and the failure does not amount to a major failure.
